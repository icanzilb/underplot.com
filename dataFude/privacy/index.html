Privacy Note

Last updated: Dec 9, 2022

1 Key points

  • Processing of sensitive personal information: dataFude does not store or process personal or identifying information.
  • Anonymized usage information: dataFude collects anonymous usage data in order to prioritize future development. This data isn’t related in any way to the users who generate it.
  • Payments and App Store accounts: When you make a purchase in the app and/or manage your app subscription you are doing this through the services Apple offers on their macOS platform that is installed on your computer. In this process, no personal or payment information is made available to dataFude.

2 Changes

We might change this note from time to time and if we do so we will make this known through the news and updates section on our website.
