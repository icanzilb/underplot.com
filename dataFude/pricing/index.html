Pricing Note

Last updated: Mar 1, 2022

dataFude MAX

The app is distributed as a free limited version.

From the Preferences window users can purchase a MAX license for a duration of 1 year. Once the license expires it does not renew automatically and the app is usable as the free Mini version again.

Users can choose to renew the license for another year at will.

dataFude

The app is distributed as a free limited version.

From the Preferences window users can purchase a Pro subscription which can be billed either monthly or yearly. Until cancelled the plan renews automatically.

Users can choose to cancel the subscription renewal at the next billing date at any time from their Apple account.
