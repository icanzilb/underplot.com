Integrate with Xcode:

1. Copy Tile.app to your /Applications directory

2. Open your Xcode project. Click Project/Scheme/Edit Scheme...

3. Click In Run/Pre-actions/+ to add new pre-run script.

4. In the text field enter open /Applications/Tile.app --args "Name of your app".

5. Run the project like normal. Check out the demo app in the download file for a small example.
